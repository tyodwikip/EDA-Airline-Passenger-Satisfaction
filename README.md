# EDA-Airline-Passenger-Satisfaction

Capstone Project Module 2: Taking a look closer at insight from the airline satisfaction survey data to identify key areas of improvement that will have the strongest impact on passenger satisfaction.

Insight: 
* The number of passenger who satisfied are 58697 (56.7%) while Neutral/Dissatisfied are 44897 (43.6%). There is sastisfaction gap about 13,1%
* Most of the passengers who flew in Economy Plus were dissatisfied with the flight, and those who flew in Business Class were satisfied
* Top 5 highest correlation to satisfaction: online boarding with 0.55, inflight entertainment 0.39, seat comfort with 0.36, on-board service with 0.32 and leg-room service with 0.31

Plausible Action:
* Shifting customer segment to personal travel and economy class
* Focus on improving the Inflight Wi-Fi Service, Online boarding, Inflight entertainment, Seat comfort, On-board service and Leg room service
